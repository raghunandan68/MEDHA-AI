# User Manual
**Project Name:** Medha-AI  

---

## 1. Introduction
Welcome to Medha-AI! Your personal AI study companion. This guide will walk you through setting up your account, uploading your first document, and generating study materials.

## 2. Login Guide
1. Navigate to `www.medha-ai.com` in your web browser.
2. Click **Sign Up** in the top right corner.
3. You can either:
   - Enter your Email and create a Password.
   - Click **Continue with Google** for one-click access.
4. If you used email, verify your account via the link sent to your inbox.
5. Once logged in, you will be directed to your **Dashboard**.

## 3. Feature Usage

### Uploading a Document
1. On the Dashboard, locate the **Upload Area**.
2. Drag and drop your PDF, DOCX, or TXT file into the box, or click to browse files.
3. Wait for the progress bar to complete. The system is extracting text and making it AI-ready.
4. Your document will appear in the **My Documents** list.

### Generating Flashcards
1. Click on a document from your list to open it.
2. In the right panel, select the **Flashcards** tab.
3. Click **Generate New Flashcards**. Choose the number of cards (e.g., 10).
4. Review the cards. Click a card to flip it and reveal the answer.

### Taking a Quiz
1. Select the **Quiz** tab.
2. Choose your difficulty level (Easy, Medium, Hard).
3. Answer the multiple-choice questions.
4. Submit the quiz to view your score and detailed explanations for any incorrect answers.

### Using the AI Chatbot
1. Select the **Chat** tab.
2. Type a question related to your document in the chat box. (e.g., "What are the three main causes of the French Revolution mentioned in chapter 2?")
3. The AI will provide an answer based *strictly* on your uploaded text.

## 4. Screens Explanation
- **Dashboard:** Your home base. Shows recent files and quick upload links.
- **Document Workspace:** A split-view screen. Read your document on the left, use AI tools on the right.
- **Profile Settings:** Update your name, change your password, or manage your subscription plan.

## 5. Troubleshooting Guide
| Problem | Solution |
|---|---|
| File won't upload | Ensure the file is under 50MB and is a PDF, DOCX, or TXT. Scanned PDFs (images) might not parse text correctly. |
| Flashcards are blank | The document might contain mostly images. Ensure your file has selectable text. |
| Chatbot says "I don't know" | The chatbot is programmed not to guess. If the answer isn't in your document, it won't answer. Try rephrasing your question. |

## 6. FAQ Section
**Q: Is my data private?**  
A: Yes. Your documents are stored securely and are only accessible by your account. We do not use your private documents to train public AI models.

**Q: Can I export my flashcards?**  
A: Yes, you can click "Export to CSV" in the Flashcards tab to use them in Anki or Quizlet.

**Q: Is there a mobile app?**  
A: Medha-AI is fully responsive and works perfectly on mobile browsers. A dedicated app is on our roadmap.
